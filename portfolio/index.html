EL SEPTIMO -- GENEVA

Geneva -- International Luxury Cigar Brand

Restricted Category↓Rolls Royce DealMeta Ads policy workaround -- High-value B2B acquisition
Meta Ads Policy WorkaroundLuxury Brand StrategyHigh-Value B2B AcquisitionContent Strategy

El Septimo is a Geneva-based ultra-premium cigar brand with a product lineup that includes some of the most exclusive cigars in the world. The challenge: Meta Ads prohibits direct tobacco advertising, making paid acquisition nearly impossible for most cigar brands. We engineered a compliant campaign architecture -- using lifestyle positioning, brand storytelling, and Meta interest-based targeting to reach ultra-high-net-worth audiences without triggering tobacco policy restrictions. The campaign generated significant high-value brand exposure and directly contributed to securing a commercial relationship with Rolls Royce -- one of the brand's highest-profile partnerships. This case demonstrates our capability to navigate complex ad platform restrictions while still delivering measurable premium results.
