The Story

BUILT FROM THE GROUND UP IN SCHAUMBURG.

Precision Digital Marketing was built specifically for local businesses in the Chicago western suburbs. Not national brands. Not e-commerce. Local businesses that serve real communities and need marketing that understands their market.

After watching too many good local businesses get burned by generalist agencies that applied national strategies to hyper-local problems, Aamir built PDM around a different model: deep specialization in local markets, direct access to the person running your account, and a results-first approach with no long-term contracts.

The client roster includes HVAC companies, roofing contractors, fitness studios, luxury goods brands, cigar companies, catering businesses, and service businesses across Schaumburg, Naperville, Oak Brook, and the broader Chicago suburbs.
